Obscurity

  • Home

Obscurity follows the life of a 15 year old boy, called Josh.
The inspiring tale follows Josh's story as his new-found confidence changes his life completely.

Featured in The Weston Mercury and premiered at Priory Community School, Obscurity is the new film from 9eight Films and Grant Productions.

About the film

Quick turnaround

Obscurity was created with a very quick turnaround. It was written, filmed and edited in three months.

Pre-production

Obscurity was written by George Grant in just over a month.

Production and post

Obscurity was filmed and edited by Matt Filer in about two months.
